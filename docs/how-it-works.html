How It Works

Three steps to
smarter betting

Set up a game in seconds and let Nassau handle the rest. Here's how it all comes together.

1

Create a Game

Pick your bet type, set the stakes, invite your friends, and choose your course. Nassau handles the setup so you can focus on warming up.

  • Choose Nassau, Skins, or Match Play bet types
  • Set per-bet dollar amounts and press limits
  • Invite friends from your friends list
  • Select a course for automatic par loading
  • Configure handicap strokes for fair play
2

Play and Score

Enter scores hole by hole as you play. Nassau tracks every bet, press, and standing in real time across all players.

  • Tap to enter scores with par-relative coloring
  • Live bet standings update after every hole
  • Initiate presses when you're down with one tap
  • Get AI-powered press advice with Nassau Pro
  • Add late players before hole 2
3

Settle Up

When the round is over, Nassau calculates exactly what everyone owes. Send payments with a single tap.

  • Automatic payout calculations across all bets
  • One-tap Venmo deep links for instant payment
  • Track which settlements are paid vs unpaid
  • Post-round AI analysis with Nassau Pro
  • Full game history and settlement records
Bet Types

Pick your game

Nassau supports multiple bet formats so you can play the way you like.

Classic

Nassau

Three bets in one: front 9, back 9, and overall 18. The gold standard of golf betting with optional presses to double the action.

Per Hole

Skins

Win a skin by having the lowest score on a hole. Ties carry over to the next hole, building up the pot for dramatic finishes.

Head to Head

Match Play

Win holes, not strokes. Each hole is a separate contest. The player who wins the most holes takes the match.

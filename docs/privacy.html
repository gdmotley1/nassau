Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Nassau ("we", "our", "us") is a golf betting app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and optional handicap when you create an account.
  • Game Data: Scores, bet amounts, game history, and settlement records you enter while using the app.
  • Payment Identifiers: Venmo username (optional) for settlement deep links. We do not process or store payment credentials.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.
  • Subscription Data: Purchase receipts are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We store your subscription status but not payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Nassau app and its features.
  • To track your game history, statistics, and performance insights.
  • To facilitate settlements between players.
  • To send push notifications about game updates and invitations.
  • To manage your subscription through RevenueCat.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses enterprise-grade PostgreSQL databases with row-level security. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management.
  • Apple: In-app purchases and App Store services.
  • Expo: Push notification delivery.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Game data (scores, results) is shared only with other players in your games. We do not share your information with advertisers.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at support@nassauapp.com.

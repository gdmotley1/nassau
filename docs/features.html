Features

Everything you need
on the course

From first tee to the 19th hole, Nassau handles the math so you can focus on your game.

⛳

Live Scorecard

Real-time scoring with automatic front 9, back 9, and overall 18 bet tracking for every player.

  • Score entry with par-relative coloring
  • Automatic bet standings after every hole
  • Support for 2-4 players per game
  • Course par auto-fill from saved courses
💰

Instant Settlements

Automatic payout calculations with one-tap Venmo deep links. No more arguing over who owes what.

  • Auto-calculated payouts for every bet
  • One-tap Venmo payment links
  • Track paid vs unpaid settlements
  • Full settlement history
⚡

Press Bets

Initiate presses mid-round with automatic doubling. Configurable press limits protect your wallet.

  • Press any bet region mid-round
  • Automatic double-or-nothing calculation
  • Configurable press limits (1, 2, 3, or unlimited)
  • Press confirmation with dollar amounts
👥

Friends System

Add friends by code, invite them to games, and track head-to-head records over time.

  • Unique 6-character friend codes
  • One-tap friend invites to new games
  • Head-to-head records and P/L tracking
  • Late join before hole 2
📈

Lifetime Stats

Track your P/L over time with interactive charts, win rate, streaks, and game type breakdowns.

  • Interactive monthly P/L chart
  • Win rate, streak, and best month stats
  • Game type performance breakdown
  • Performance insights and trends
🏌

Course Memory

Save course pars once and they auto-fill for every future round. Smart course autocomplete.

  • Save full 18-hole par data per course
  • Smart autocomplete when typing course name
  • Auto-fill pars for repeat courses
  • 20+ Georgia courses pre-loaded

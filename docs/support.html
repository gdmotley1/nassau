FAQ

How do I settle up after a round?

When a game is completed, tap "Settle Up" on the game detail screen. Nassau will calculate what each player owes and provide one-tap Venmo links for easy payment.

How do I add friends?

Go to your Profile, tap Friends, then share your friend code or enter a friend's code to connect. Friends appear when creating new games.

What is Nassau Pro?

Nassau Pro is our premium subscription that unlocks AI-powered features including press advice, matchup analytics, and post-round analysis to help you bet smarter.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find Nassau and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at the email below and we'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

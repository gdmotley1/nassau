Getting Started

How do I create a game?

Tap the + button in the tab bar. Choose your game type, set your bet amount and press limits, select the course, then invite friends. The whole setup takes about 30 seconds.

How do I add friends?

Go to your Profile tab, tap Friends, then share your unique 6-character friend code or enter a friend's code to connect. Once added, friends automatically appear when you create new games.

Can I join a game that's already started?

Yes. Players can late-join any game before hole 2 scoring begins. The game creator can add players from the game lobby, and all bets will be calculated from hole 1.

Betting & Scoring

How do I settle up after a round?

When all 18 holes are scored, tap "Settle Up" on the game screen. Nassau calculates exactly what each player owes, then generates one-tap Venmo deep links so you can pay or request money instantly.

How do press bets work?

A press creates a new bet within the current region (front 9, back 9, or overall) starting from the current hole. It's essentially a double-or-nothing side bet. You can press any active bet, and Nassau handles all the payout math automatically.

Press limits are configurable when creating the game (1, 2, 3, or unlimited per region) so nobody gets in over their head.

What bet types are supported?

Nassau currently supports the classic Nassau format: front 9, back 9, and overall 18, with optional press bets on each. Skins, Wolf, and Match Play are coming soon.

Nassau Pro

What is Nassau Pro?

Nassau Pro is our premium subscription that unlocks AI-powered betting intelligence. It includes pre-round course scouting, real-time press advice with win-rate calculations, opponent matchup analytics, group leaderboards, and shareable stats cards.

Think of it this way: the free version is the tool. Pro is the edge. Learn more

How much does Pro cost?

$4.99/month, $29.99/year (save 50%), or $79.99 for lifetime access. All plans unlock every Pro feature instantly. Cancel anytime.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Nassau and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access through the end of your current billing period.

Account & Privacy

How do I delete my account?

Email us at support@nassauapp.com with the subject "Delete Account" and we'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What data does Nassau collect?

Only what's needed to run the app: your email, display name, game scores, and betting data. We never sell your data to third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Is my payment information secure?

Nassau never stores credit card information. All subscription payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store. Settlement payments are handled directly through Venmo's own app.
